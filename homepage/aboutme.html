About Me

Welcome to Nala's gallery! I don't have many hobbies and none of them is fancy. However, when I need a break, I do rely on reading books, watching movies/TV series, writing, drawing, cooking, and exercising at different stages of my life. They have helped me focus on what I was doing while untangling my knotted thoughts. I appreciate all these hobbies that I have picked up on my way exploring the world and life. I cherished all memories that they brought to me, physically or mentally. Shared on this site are some pieces of them.
